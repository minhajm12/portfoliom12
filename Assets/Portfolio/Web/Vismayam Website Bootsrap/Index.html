-Since 1999-

Vismayam (VCAM), the first and foremost design training institute in Kerala, is now upgraded as an international study centre. VIsmayam is one of the certified training partners of IPD-OUM (Professional Development-Open University Malaysia), offering diploma programs in multimedia and Interior design to the students of 10th grade and growing professionals. 1000 + students successfully completed their graduation in Interior design and Multimedia communication by choosing VCAM as the training partner under university of Calicut. Department of Architecture

One of the main attractions of the interior design course at Vismayam College is its academic standards for monitoring the same. Experienced faculty members and course coordinators make sure the right propagation of course content to the candidates at right time. Periodical visits of industry experts from the core field of architecture and interior design are inspiring students to achieve their dream career straightforwardly.Our Architecture Department offers opening to the students for participating national events, Industrial Visits, Workshops, Exhibitions, etc in the course period. It helps our students to build their professional competence during and after the course.

Multimedia and communication

A well-organized system for monitoring the students in their theory, practical and studio sessions making multimedia course at Vismayam the best multimedia course in Calicut. Providing Special sessions by experts from the Industry, periodical industrial visits, Workshops, public events, placement support, etc are the other reasons for students to choose vismayam as the best multimedia centre in Calicut. Courses offered in multimedia by the department of Media and communication is in three different levels (One year certificate course in multimedia, Two year Executive diploma in Multimedia, Three Year professional Diploma in Multimedia).
